Coping

Purpose

    Coping is different for everyone, and someone may be coping in an unhealthy way. Sometimes it is easy to notice, such as cutting, sometimes it is less obvious such as seeking attention.

    The goal of healthy coping mechanisms is to directly stop the unhealthy coping and replace it with beneficial yet effective methods. Find other ways to safely take out anger, frustration, sadness, and other negative emotions.

Unhealthy Mechanisms and their Alternatives

Unhealthy Methods

    Self Harm.

    Zoning Out.

    Over Sleeping.

    Doing Drugs/Alcohol.

    Isolation.

    Fighting.

Healthier Methods

    Using red-dyed ice to rub against areas you wish to cut.

    Finding something productive and unrelated to stress factors to focus on.

    Finding ways to fill your schedule(hang with friends, community events, activies, etc).

    Find a hobby you can enjoy.

    Pushing yourself to speak to people. Online can be an easier way for many people.

    Throwing ice at the ground, flatten cans, hit trees with sticks, ask a friend to pillow fight.
