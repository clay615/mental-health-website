Purpose of Hotlines

    These hotlines are at your disposal, whether in an emergency or not. If you are experiencing any type of abuse at any age, including but not limited to physical, sexual, and mental abuse, do not hesitate to contact one or more of the hotlines provided above in the abuse section. Almost all of these lines are free and confidental, and have a quick exit method to leave the site if you are in danger.

    These lines are not psychologists and are not fit to diagnose you, however they are there for you if you are in a crisis or need to talk to someone. There are more lines than these, but here are some if you are currently in a crisis.

    If you are currently going through an overdose, or with someone who is overdosing, do not call a hotline, instead call poison control for immediate advice, and 911 for additional help. If you have narcan on hand, use it to delay the immediate effects and call 911.
