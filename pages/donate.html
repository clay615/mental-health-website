Benefits of Donating to Charities/Research benefits

    Before you donate, you probably want to know what your money will be helping with. When you donate money to these charities, you are helping fund treatment for those who suffer from mental illnesses. Your money can also be used to research better treatment options for many different mental illnesses.

    The money you give to charities is used to help pay for peoples access to free mental health services, such as hotlines, therapy, and sponserships. The money given to research is used to help advance our knowledge about mental disorders, as well as treat it.

    If you are interested in donating, you can check out one of the organizations listed in our "Interested in Donating?" section.
