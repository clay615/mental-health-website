PTSD(Post Traumatic Stress Disorder)

       PTSD causes the re-living of very traumatic moments. Triggers, such as loud sounds, bright bursts of light, and other sudden events can trigger the PTSD. PTSD can cause those suffering from it to be hypervigilant, blame themself for the traumatic event, not trust others, and emotional outbursts.

What Causes PTSD

       PTSD is typically caused by traumatizing memories and brain injuries. Some common causes of PTSD are assault, horrific scenes, combat or war, and the sudden death of loved ones.

Demographics Affected

       Roughly 3.9% of people have PTSD. One of the msot common causes of PTSD is war. Many people in war will see gruesome sights, get badly injured, or go through another scary event, which can cause PTSD. Another leading cause is PTSD is car accidents. Car accidents can happen suddenly, and can be very dangerous, which is why it can lead to car crash victims having PTSD.
