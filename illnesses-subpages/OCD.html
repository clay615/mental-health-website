OCD(Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder)

       OCD is the need for everything to be in a certain order, done in a certain way, or done a certain amount of times. OCD can cause a fear of losing control of oneself, over aggression, fear of germs, doubting themself, and persistent unwanted thoughts.

What Causes OCD

       Neurological trauma and genetics can cause a person to develop OCD. It can be worsened, or triggered by environmental triggers, such as trauma, stress, or childhood events.

Demographics Affected

       About 2.5% of the population is affected with OCD, with men making up 2/5th of that, and women making up the other 3/5th of that.
